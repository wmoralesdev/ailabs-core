# Ai Labs

Working source of truth for what Ai Labs is, who it serves, and what it offers.

This document is brand and substance only. Website structure and open sales decisions live elsewhere.

## What Ai Labs Is

Ai Labs helps companies adapt, develop, and learn with AI.

We are based in El Salvador and work with teams across Central America and beyond.

We do that through three pillars:

- **Academy** — teaching: consultancies, workshops, bootcamps, and residencies so people can learn AI in practice.
- **Agentic** — building: software development on demand, plus workflow and automation work, done with AI.
- **Aperture** — connecting: a community and innovation hub that links people, profiles we work with, and the companies we represent or partner with.

The community sits under all three. It is not a product line. It is how trust, talent, and opportunities move through the work.

## The Three Pillars

Order does not matter in public materials. Use the pillar that fits the conversation. Always pair the name with a plain description nearby.

### Academy — Learn with us

Academy is the teaching side of Ai Labs.

It includes:

- Consultancies and workshops for companies and teams.
- Bootcamps and residencies when the format fits.
- Practical training for founders, operators, students, and job seekers.

Programs may be paid by companies, sponsors, universities, or participants, depending on the format.

Current program examples:

- **Claude for Entrepreneurs** — one-day intensive for founders and operators.
- **Get Competitive Quick** — full-day experience for students and job seekers looking for opportunities beyond El Salvador.
- **AI and Automation** — training for companies that want to scale operations.

Working line:

> Ai Labs Academy helps people and teams learn AI through practice, not theory dumps.

Avoid:

- Sounding like a generic bootcamp brand.
- Teaching tools with no real application.
- Treating Academy as disconnected from the builder community.

### Agentic — Build with us

Agentic is software development on demand, done with AI.

It also covers workflow and automation work when that is what the company needs.

Typical work:

- Products and MVPs.
- Internal tools.
- Workflow automation.
- Operational systems.
- Iteration and technical partnership when teams need AI product judgment.

Primary audiences:

- Startups building a product.
- Companies looking for a digital partner.
- Investors with concepts or MVPs they want to take further.

Working line:

> Ai Labs Agentic builds software and workflow systems for teams that need to move from idea to something usable.

Avoid:

- Framing Agentic only as "AI agents."
- Sounding like staff augmentation.
- Overselling autonomous systems before scope is clear.

### Aperture — Connect with us

Aperture is the community and innovation hub.

It has two sides that work together:

1. **People** — connect participants with other profiles in the network, and bring them closer to the companies Ai Labs represents or partners with.
2. **Partners** — help sponsors, companies, and institutions engage the ecosystem through programs, events, credits when available, and introductions.

What Aperture can offer when relevant:

- Direction on how to engage the AI builder ecosystem.
- Access to the Ai Labs network.
- Introductions to builders, partners, and represented brands when appropriate.
- Community programming and facilitation.
- Credits and opportunities when they are available.

Working line:

> Ai Labs Aperture connects people and companies to the AI community, partner network, and opportunities around it.

Avoid:

- Promising guaranteed access to specific brands, people, credits, or outcomes.
- Becoming a vague "innovation" bucket.
- Treating community access like a mailing list.

Guardrail:

Aperture can promise direction, programming, facilitation, and relevant introductions. It cannot promise outcomes controlled by third parties.
